A lot of devs start their journey with simple 'Hello Worlds!' and some basic HTML/CSS, and I am no different. My first 'Hello World' was actually a batch script I wrote using Notepad and the Windows Command Prompt. Programming was interesting, but much too daunting to actually tackle without a mentor. In high school, I took some HTML/CSS and really dove into web design. After being the webmaster for my student government in college, I gained a little more respect for technology and programming. Eventually, I got to Lambda School and began my journey with a strong foot forward!
